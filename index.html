About Rohyp-no

What is Rohyp-no?

Rohyp-no is a roofie detecting stick that detects whether any drug has been mixed with an alcoholic/non-alcoholic drink. It looks like a stirrer to make it more discrete and subtle. It can detect three main types of date drugs, gamma-hydroxybutyric acid, Rohypnol (roofie), and ketamine.

How does Rohyp-no work?

When dipped in the drink, it displays the results in 30 seconds. If the colour changes, the drug has been tampered with, if not, it is safe to drink.

What does Rohyp-no mean?

Rohyp-no is a play on the word Rohypnol or Roofie. We as a community stand strongly against the heinous act of spiking drinks. Rohyp-NO!
